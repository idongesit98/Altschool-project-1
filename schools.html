About AltSchools

AltSchools is a cutting-edge institution offering specialized courses across various schools, including the School of Engineering, School of Product, and School of Data. Each school provides a diverse range of courses designed to equip students with the skills needed to excel in the tech industry.

School Details

School of Engineering (SOE): Focuses on developing technical skills in frontend and backend development, cloud technologies, and cyber security.

School of Product (SOP): Offers courses in product design, marketing, and management, providing students with a comprehensive understanding of product development and market strategies.

School of Data (SOD): Specializes in data science, data analysis, and data engineering, enabling students to harness the power of data for informed decision-making.
